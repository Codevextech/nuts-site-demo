Our Story

From Farm to Your Family

Founded in 2021, NutriNuts began with a simple mission: to make premium quality, organic dry fruits accessible to everyone in India. Disheartened by the chemical-laden, stale nuts available in regular supermarkets, our founders set out to source directly from the farmers.

We travel across the finest orchards — from the almond valleys of California to the walnut farms of Kashmir — to handpick the best harvest. By cutting out the middlemen, we ensure that the farmers get a fair price and you get the freshest product possible.

Today, we serve over 10,000+ happy families who trust us for their daily dose of health.
